🌿 The Real PDX OG Review Criteria

We don’t rate cannabis like it’s wine or perfume. We rate it like it’s experience. Every strain we review is judged on three core pillars: Smell, Flavor, and Expansion. Here’s how we break it down:

👃 Smell

We judge aroma on intensity, complexity, and character. The best strains hit your nose like a memory—bold, earthy, and unmistakable.

What we love:

  • Pungent, nose-tingling terps
  • Earthy aromas: plants, spices, foods, even animals (yes, we love a good skunk)
  • That OG homegrown kick-ass funk

What we don’t:

  • Weak or absent smell
  • Wet hay, wet grass
  • Plastic, burnt plastic, or indescribable chemical funk

👅 Flavor / Taste

Taste isn’t just about the inhale—it’s the full arc: inhale, exhale, and aftertaste. We want flavor that lingers, evolves, and tells a story.

What we love:

  • Bold earthy notes: spices, sweets, sours, plants, foods
  • A flavor journey from draw to exhale

What we don’t:

  • Bland or muted taste
  • Plastic or burnt plastic
  • Chemical weirdness that defies description

🫁 Expansion

This one’s harder to explain—but once you feel it, you know. Expansion is the sensation of the smoke growing inside your chest, like it’s still flowing even after you stop inhaling. It’s a chest hug, a chest bump, a moment where the weed says:

“No need to hold me in—I’m good s**t.”

What it’s not:

  • Harsh throat burn
  • Coughing fits
  • That dry, scratchy irritation from bad weed

Expansion is a physical and chemical reaction—some feel it more than others. But when it hits right, it’s unforgettable.
