🔥 About The Real PDX OG

Here at The Real PDX OG, we’ve got a dedicated crew of seasoned smokers who live for the experience—and we’re here to deliver the realest, rawest cannabis reviews you’ll find in today’s dispensaries.

We don’t hold back. Every review is built on honesty, not hype. You might not always agree with us (what works for one person might flop for another), and that’s okay—we give you the chance to speak up. Every strain comes with a vote: Agree or Disagree, so your voice helps shape the community.

We’re here to share our wisdom and lived experience. But remember: cannabis is complex. Not all plants are equal. Even clones can vary wildly depending on how they’re grown. Conditions shift, batches change, and the final product can surprise you—for better or worse.

That’s why we track the details that matter: harvest dates, growers, producers, dispensaries, and more. Our goal is to spotlight consistency and quality, so you can make the most informed choices possible.

Yes, we’re “Weed Snobs”—but it’s all about the experience. Think of it like this: you can drive across town in a beat-up old car, or you can ride in a fully catered limo. Both get you there. But one makes the journey unforgettable.

Great cannabis can do that. The taste, the aroma, the feeling—it’s not just about getting high. It’s about elevating the ride.

And if you’re wondering what sets us apart—we’re the ones handing you the little pink spoon. Just like that first taste at the ice cream counter, we serve up real samples of the cannabis experience. No pressure, no fluff—just a flavorful scoop of what’s good, what’s mid, and what’s straight-up legendary.
